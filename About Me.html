About Me

Hello! My name is Oscar Rashed. I am a sophomore at Gonzaga University, where I am both studying and competing as a collegiate tennis player. Though I grew up in Switzerland, my roots span across France, Italy, Lebanon, and Egypt, giving me a rich and diverse cultural background.

At 15, I made the bold decision to move to Barcelona, Spain, to chase my dream of becoming a Division 1 tennis player. This journey not only shaped me as an athlete but also as an individual, instilling in me resilience, discipline, and an unwavering commitment to my goals.

Along the way, I have embraced the beauty of languages, becoming fluent in four. Beyond the court, I find joy in playing the guitar, immersing myself in books, and spending quality time with friends.
